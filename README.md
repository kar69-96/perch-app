# Welcome to Perch!

Perch is a little buddy that lives in your MacBook notch and helps you whenever you need it.

It sees your screen, talks back, and can go do things for you — right there at the top
of your display. Hold `⌃ Control + ⌥ Option` to talk, or tap **Control** twice to type.

**macOS only** — works best on a MacBook with a notch, running **macOS 14.2 or newer** (Apple Silicon).
